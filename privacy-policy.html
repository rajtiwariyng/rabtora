Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 Jan, 2026

At Rabtora Madhyam Management, based in the United Arab Emirates (UAE), we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data in accordance with applicable UAE data protection laws, including the UAE Personal Data Protection Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021).

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and any other information you provide via contact forms, inquiries, or direct communication.
  • Business & Project Information: Branding details, marketing requirements, website or advertising briefs, and event-related information shared for service delivery.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device data, pages visited, and usage statistics collected through cookies or analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested services
  • To communicate regarding projects, proposals, or service updates
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience
  • For internal analysis, reporting, and business operations
  • For marketing communications (only where consent is provided)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality, analyze traffic, and understand user behavior. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers such as:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Email and communication platforms

All third parties are required to comply with data protection standards and use your information only for authorized purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. While we strive to protect your information, no online transmission is completely secure.

6. International Data Transfers

Where necessary, your information may be transferred or stored outside the UAE. In such cases, we ensure adequate data protection measures are in place in compliance with applicable laws.

7. Your Rights

Under UAE data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Object to certain processing activities

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
